Why we do what we do

We Olive has been committed to local farmers for two decades and our passion for locally sourced, healthy, delicious, artisan extra virgin olive oil has only grown. We love sharing our passion with you! When a customer visits one of our locations, it is our ultimate goal to pass along that passion and to instill the excitement and celebration of fresh, healthy foods made with ingredients from local artisans and farmers. Our first location opened in La Jolla 20 years ago this year.

Come celebrate this milestone anniversary with us by visiting one of our eight locations. Working with these local artisans to market their products is what the We Olive brand is all about. Keeping true to this, We Olive only sells California Olive Oil Council (COOC) approved California produced Extra Virgin Olive Oils. On a daily basis, we give customers the opportunity to discover California’s finest Extra Virgin Olive Oil, and we’re proud to do so! When a consumer walks through our doors, or visits our website, we make an effort to teach them as much as they’d like to know.

From the history of EVOO and how to choose the right one, to its many culinary applications and the FDA approved heart healthy benefits, We Olive consumers are informed consumers.

When you shop at the We Olive online olive oil store, you can rest assured you will receive only certified, first cold-pressed extra virgin olive oil! Besides, you are supporting the local artisan-made extra virgin olive oil producers in California. The unique weather and terrain in this part of the US create ideal conditions only comparable to the Mediterranean region. Thus, we can enjoy the extraordinary flavor and health benefits that can only come from Authentic Extra Virgin Olive Oil! EVOO - olive oil at it's finest.

While the California wine industry has been popular around the world for decades, California's EVOO industry is still relatively unknown. Even though California's olive oil has been recognized internationally for its superior flavor and craftsmanship, many people are unaware of its existence.

Reservations

Patio

The top patio is a lively and stunning seating area. During the cooler winter months the area is cozy with heaters to make sure the gorgeous sunset can be enjoyed all year round. In the warmer months our large umbrellas keep the patio a cool temperature for our guests. From this top patio, you will be able to enjoy the gorgeous ocean views while sipping your favorite wine.

Bar

Cozy and intimate, the bar area offers guests a personal experience no matter the weather. With high top tables and bar seating, guests will be immersed in our warm living-room like space nestled between the top patio area and the tasting room and gormet food section. The bar area is inside, though not too far from the ocean breeze thanks to our picturesque french doors.

Tasting Room

Our tasting room can be booked only during the hours of 12-4pm and gives guests a private environment to taste and experience our wins and olive oils. Our staff is happy to tailor tasting to fit your or your groups needs! Most wine tasting last between 45 mins-60 mins and can be reserved for groups between 1-8 people. We are more than happy to accomodate special requests. Please call us if you have any questions.
